Himlayang Cemetery General Trias

The Himlayang General Trias, a memorial park owned and developed by the Municipality of General Trias was blessed in a simple ceremony last January 3, 2011. The blessing ceremony led by Mayor Luis A. Ferrer IV, was attended by local officials and department heads of the municipality and officiated by Rev. Fr. Calixto Lumandas. The memorial park aims to address the growing needs of the constituents by offering services and amenities at par with private contemporaries at a much lower costs. The marketing of the memorial park was tasked to the Investment Promotion Office under Mr. Romel D. Olimpo while the maintenance will be supervised by the Engineering Office under Engr. Norman Bugtong. The memorial park is another proof of the local governmentâ€™s continuous effort to provide better service to the people.
